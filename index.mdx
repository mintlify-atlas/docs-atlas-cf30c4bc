## What is Elite Skyblock?

Elite Skyblock gives Hypixel SkyBlock farmers a single place to track their progress and compare against other players. The platform pulls live data from Hypixel's API to calculate your farming weight — a composite score based on crop collections, farming level, contest medals, tier-12 minions, and more.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Search for a player">
    Go to [eliteskyblock.com](https://eliteskyblock.com) and type any Hypixel username into the search bar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse your profile">
    View your farming weight, crop collections, skills, garden stats, fortune breakdown, and active event memberships.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check the leaderboards">
    See where you rank globally for farming weight, individual crops, Jacob medals, and more.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Join a community event">
    Team up with other players in seasonal farming events for rewards and recognition.
  </Step>
</Steps>
