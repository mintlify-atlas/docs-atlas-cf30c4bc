---
title: "Quick Start"
description: "Look up a Hypixel SkyBlock player, read their farming profile, and check their leaderboard rank in a few steps."
---

Elite Skyblock requires no account to use. You can look up any Hypixel SkyBlock player by username and immediately see their farming stats.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Search for a player">
    Go to [eliteskyblock.com](https://eliteskyblock.com) and type a Hypixel username into the search bar on the home page.

    You can also navigate directly to a player's profile by URL:

    ```
    https://eliteskyblock.com/@{username}
    ```

    For example, `https://eliteskyblock.com/@Technoblade` opens Technoblade's profile.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select a SkyBlock profile">
    If the player has multiple SkyBlock profiles, the site loads their most recently active one by default. Use the profile selector at the top of the page to switch between profiles.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Read the profile tabs">
    Each profile has the following tabs:

    | Tab | What it shows |
    |-----|---------------|
    | **Stats** | Farming weight breakdown, crop collections, skills, and general farming stats |
    | **Garden** | Garden level, plot upgrades, and visitor history |
    | **Fortune** | Current farming fortune and the cheapest available upgrades |
    | **Contests** | Full Jacob's contest history, brackets entered, and medals earned |
    | **Ranks** | The player's leaderboard position across farming weight and individual crops |
    | **Charts** | Historical graphs of farming weight and collection growth over time |

    Start with **Stats** to see the player's overall farming weight and how it breaks down by crop.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Check leaderboard rank">
    Open the **Ranks** tab to see where the player stands on the global leaderboards. Each crop, the overall farming weight score, and Jacob's contest medals each have their own leaderboard.

    You can also browse the full leaderboard at [eliteskyblock.com/leaderboard/farmingweight](https://eliteskyblock.com/leaderboard/farmingweight) to see the top farmers globally.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  The Elite Farmers Discord bot lets you access stats and leaderboards without leaving Discord. Use commands like `/weight`, `/rates`, and `/leaderboard` after inviting the bot to your server.
</Tip>
