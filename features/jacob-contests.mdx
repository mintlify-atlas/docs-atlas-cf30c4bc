Jacob's Farming Contest is a recurring Hypixel SkyBlock event where players compete to collect as many of a specific crop as possible within a time window. Elite Skyblock tracks contest results, bracket cutoffs, and medal history for all recorded contests and players.

## Browsing contests

Visit [eliteskyblock.com/contests](https://eliteskyblock.com/contests) to access the contest archive.

### Navigating the archive

The contest browser lets you search by SkyBlock year, year and month, or a specific year/month/day combination. Each contest entry shows:

- The SkyBlock timestamp of the contest
- The crop or crops contested
- Bracket cutoff scores (Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum, Diamond)
- Rankings of all known participants and their scores

## Player contest history

To view a specific player's full contest history, go to the Contests tab on their profile page:

```
https://eliteskyblock.com/@{username}/{profileName}/contests
```

You can also search for a player directly from [eliteskyblock.com/contests](https://eliteskyblock.com/contests) using the player search form.

The Contests tab shows:

- Total participation count
- Medal breakdown (Diamond, Platinum, Gold, Silver, Bronze) for each SkyBlock year and overall
- Every individual contest, grouped by year, with the crop, score, and medal earned
- Toggle between SkyBlock time and real-world time for contest timestamps

## Medals and farming weight

Contest medals contribute to [Farming Weight](/features/farming-weight). Diamond, Platinum, and Gold medals each add bonus weight, up to a maximum of 1,000 medals counted toward the bonus. Earning higher-tier medals is one of the more impactful ways to increase farming weight beyond raw crop collection.

| Medal | Weight per medal (up to 1,000 counted) |
|---|---|
| Diamond | 0.75 |
| Platinum | 0.5 |
| Gold | 0.25 |

<Info>
  Silver and Bronze medals are tracked and displayed on the profile, but do not contribute to Farming Weight.
</Info>

## Medal leaderboards

Players can be ranked by the number of each medal type they have earned. These leaderboards are available on the [Leaderboards](/features/leaderboards) page under the General category.
