The Elite Shop sells cosmetic items and premium perks for your public Elite Skyblock profile. All items are cosmetic — they do not affect farming weight, leaderboard rankings, or gameplay in any way.

The shop is available at:

```
https://eliteskyblock.com/shop
```

<Note>
  You must be logged in with Discord to make a purchase. Payments are processed securely through [Tebex](https://tebex.io).
</Note>

## What's available

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Badges" icon="award">
    Badges displayed alongside your name on your public profile page.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Profile styles and themes" icon="brush">
    Custom styles and themes that change the appearance of your public profile page. Different styles affect how your profile is presented to other players.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

Items are organized into collections and featured sections on the shop home page.

## SkyBlock Gems page

Elite Skyblock has a dedicated page for Hypixel Store SkyBlock Gems at:

```
https://eliteskyblock.com/gems
```

This page is a convenience hub for Hypixel Store purchases — all transactions go through the official Hypixel Store, not through Elite Skyblock. The page shows:

- **SkyBlock Gem packages** — Direct links to gem bundles on the Hypixel Store
- **Booster Cookie pricing** — Current auction house market price for Booster Cookies, with a coins-per-gem comparison
- **Firesales** — Current limited-time Hypixel Store firesale items and their gem prices
- **Seasonal Bundle** — The current seasonal bundle items and gem costs
- **Taylor's Collection** — Taylor's Collection items and gem costs

<Tip>
  Use the creator code shown on the Gems page at the Hypixel Store for 5% off your purchase. Creator codes are the only way to get a discount on SkyBlock Gems.
</Tip>

## Purchasing an item

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in with Discord">
    Click the login button and authenticate with your Discord account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse the shop">
    Go to `https://eliteskyblock.com/shop` and browse collections or featured items.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add to cart and check out">
    Select an item and complete your purchase through Tebex. You will need enough Gems in your account, or you can purchase Gems as part of the checkout flow.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Items appear on your profile">
    After purchase, the cosmetic item or perk is applied to your public profile automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  All sales are final. Shop items are cosmetic only and do not provide any gameplay advantage or affect farming weight calculations.
</Warning>
