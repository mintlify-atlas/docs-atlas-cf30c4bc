Farming Fortune is a stat in Hypixel SkyBlock that multiplies the number of crops you receive per harvest. Higher fortune means more crops per break, which directly increases farming income and collection speed.

Elite Skyblock's Fortune Calculator reads a player's equipped gear and computes the exact fortune total for each crop, broken down by source.

## Accessing the calculator

There are two ways to use the Fortune Calculator:

  <Card title="Player profile" icon="user" href="https://eliteskyblock.com/@Kaeso/Strawberry/fortune">

  <Card title="Standalone calculator" icon="calculator" href="https://eliteskyblock.com/fortune">

  </Card>

## What the calculator shows

### Fortune progress

The calculator displays total farming fortune for each crop and tracks progress toward the maximum achievable fortune. Fortune is shown broken down by source so you can see exactly where each number comes from.

Fortune sources that are calculated include:

| Source | Examples |
|---|---|
| Farming tools | Tool level (4 fortune per level), reforge, enchantments (Harvesting, Cultivating, Dedication, etc.), Peridot gems, Farming for Dummies |
| Armor sets | Fermento, Rancher's, Squash, Cropie, and other farming armor sets |
| Equipment | Lotus equipment pieces |
| Accessories and talismans | Farming-specific accessories |
| Pets | Elephant, Mooshroom Cow, Rabbit, Bee, and other farming pets |
| Anita bonus | Bonus Farming Fortune perk levels from the Anita NPC |
| Garden milestones | Fortune bonuses unlocked at garden crop milestones |
| Plot upgrades | Per-crop fortune from garden crop upgrade levels |

### Cheapest upgrades

The calculator ranks all available fortune upgrades by cost-effectiveness — fortune gained per coin spent. This lets you identify the most efficient next step to increase your fortune without guessing.

### Rates calculator

Based on your current fortune and the selected crop and tool, the calculator estimates your expected drops per hour and approximate profit. You can adjust settings such as community center bonus and strength to refine the estimate.

<Info>
  Fortune values on the profile calculator are derived from the player's items as stored in the Hypixel API. Some temporary buffs (such as active potions) are not included because they are not visible in the API.
</Info>

## Importing settings

The fortune page supports importing settings from a share link. When another player shares their fortune configuration, you can open the link to load their settings directly into your view.

<Tip>
  Use the standalone calculator at `https://eliteskyblock.com/fortune` to experiment with gear combinations before committing to upgrades.
</Tip>
