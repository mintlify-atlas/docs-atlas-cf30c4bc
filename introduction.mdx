Elite Skyblock is the best way to check a player's farming skill and weight in Hypixel SkyBlock. The platform pulls live data from Hypixel's API to give farmers a single place to track their progress, optimize their setups, and compete against other players.

Visit the official site at [eliteskyblock.com](https://eliteskyblock.com).

## Key features

**Farming weight** — A composite score that compares farming progress across all crops using a single number. Farming weight accounts for crop collections, farming level, Jacob's contest medals, tier-12 minions, and more.

**Farming fortune** — View your current farming fortune and see the cheapest upgrades available to you. Fortune directly affects how many crops drop per harvest.

**Leaderboards** — Compete globally across farming weight, individual crops, Jacob's contest medals, and skill categories. See exactly where you rank among all tracked players.

**Jacob's contests** — Track your full contest history, bracket participation, and medal counts for any player.

**Community events** — Seasonal team-based farming competitions with leaderboards and prizes.

**Guilds** — View farming weight rankings for every member of a Hypixel guild in one place.

## Community

Join the Elite Farmers Discord community at [eliteskyblock.com/discord](https://eliteskyblock.com/discord). Full membership is unlocked after reaching a farming weight threshold. A separate support server is available for bug reports and suggestions.
